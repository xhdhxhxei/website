Anti-cheat

2024

As a self proclaimed roblox "developer", Im required to make at least one attempt at an anti-cheat. Just to explain the basic thought behind this, roblox has a really *questionable* anti-cheat, leaving developers to try and make their own. The game basically works by running a game both on the client and the server, the server asks the client what they did, and edits what the client responds with (without any protection). While there is a way to keep the game running on the server, the result is extreme lag. Which leaves us to do little custom checks to see if what is required by the client is even possible, which isnt efficient nor secure. While client sided anti cheats are possible, theyre very easily bypassed, this project is made to try and secure client sided anti cheats, and remote events. I dont trully believe this isnt bypassable, but it would take some great effort to do so.
